How Grader Works

Grader is designed to help you improve your coding skills through practice and feedback. Here's how it works:

  • Choose a problem from our extensive list of challenges.
  • Write your code to solve the problem.
  • Submit your solution for automated grading.
  • Receive instant feedback on your code efficiency and correctness.
  • Explore solutions from other users to learn new approaches.

Tips for Successful Submissions

To make the most out of your coding experience, consider the following tips:

  • Understand the problem requirements thoroughly before starting.
  • Write clean, well-documented code for better readability.
  • Test your code with various inputs to ensure its robustness.
  • Review the feedback provided to enhance your coding skills.
